# Taxonomy

This taxonomy is the proposed *commercial* packaging of what currently exists across the monorepo.

## core
Foundational TT/QTT primitives (decomposition, rounding, MPS/MPO, safety rails).

Artifacts indexed: **16**

## tci
Tensor Cross Interpolation (TT-Cross/TCI) construction and related tooling.

Artifacts indexed: **18**

## compressor
The_Compressor: production QTT compression and universal decompression stack.

Artifacts indexed: **29**

## cfd
QTT-native PDE/CFD solvers and operators (Euler/NS, shifts, spectral ops, IMEX/TDVP).

Artifacts indexed: **50**

## gpu
CUDA/Triton/GPU acceleration for QTT kernels and evaluation.

Artifacts indexed: **6**

## genesis
Genesis meta-primitives (OT/SGW/RMT/TG/RKHS/PH/GA) implemented in QTT form.

Artifacts indexed: **79**
