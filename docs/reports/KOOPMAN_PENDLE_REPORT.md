## Physical Interpretation

The unstable mode captures a **rate/reserve feedback loop**:

1. Large PT purchases temporarily drain PT reserves
2. As SY rate increases (from underlying yield), PT holders gain
3. The rate change propagates through the AMM pricing formula
4. Selling PT back captures the yield differential

This is a **yield timing arbitrage** that exploits the delay between:
- SY rate updates (continuous yield accrual)
- AMM rebalancing (discrete trades)
