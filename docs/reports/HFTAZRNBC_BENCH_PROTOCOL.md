## Procedure

### Step 1: Powder Preparation (4 hours)

**Method A: Oxide Route (Recommended)**

1. ☐ Weigh oxides in fume hood:

   | Oxide | Target (g) | Actual (g) | ✓ |
   |-------|------------|------------|---|
   | HfO₂ | 13.1 | _______ | ☐ |
   | Ta₂O₅ | 13.8 | _______ | ☐ |
   | ZrO₂ | 7.7 | _______ | ☐ |
   | Nb₂O₅ | 8.3 | _______ | ☐ |
   | Carbon | 9.0 | _______ | ☐ |

2. ☐ Combine powders in ball mill jar
3. ☐ Add ZrO₂ balls (ball:powder = 10:1 by mass)
4. ☐ Ball mill: **400 rpm, 4 hours** (alternating direction)
5. ☐ Collect homogeneous powder

**Particle size after milling:** _______ μm (target: <5 μm)

---

### Step 2: Pore Former Addition (30 minutes)

1. ☐ Weigh PMMA microspheres: 15 g
2. ☐ Add to milled powder
3. ☐ Mix gently (mortar or low-speed tumble) for 15 min

   > ⚠️ Do not ball mill — will crush the pore formers

4. ☐ Verify even distribution (visual)

---

### Step 3: Carbothermal Reduction + Sintering (8-12 hours)

**Option A: Two-Step Process**

**Step 3a: Carbothermal Reduction**

1. ☐ Load mixed powder into graphite crucible
2. ☐ Place in high-temp furnace under Ar flow
3. ☐ Temperature program:

   | Step | Temp | Rate | Hold |
   |------|------|------|------|
   | Ramp | RT → 600°C | 10°C/min | 30 min (burn out PMMA) |
   | Ramp | 600°C → 1800°C | 5°C/min | - |
   | React | 1800°C | - | 4 hours |
   | Cool | 1800°C → RT | 5°C/min | - |

4. ☐ Collect reduced powder

**XRD check:** Confirm carbide phase, no oxides remaining

**Step 3b: Spark Plasma Sintering (SPS)**

1. ☐ Load reduced powder into graphite die (30 mm)
2. ☐ Place in SPS chamber
3. ☐ Evacaute or Ar atmosphere
4. ☐ SPS parameters:

   | Parameter | Value |
   |-----------|-------|
   | Temperature | 2100°C |
   | Pressure | 30 MPa |
   | Heating rate | 100°C/min |
   | Hold time | 10 min |
   | Cooling | Furnace cool |

5. ☐ Remove sintered pellet

**Option B: Hot Press (Alternative)**

1. ☐ Load powder into graphite die
2. ☐ Hot press: 2100°C, 30 MPa, 2 hours under Ar
3. ☐ Slow cool

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Residual oxide (XRD) | Incomplete reduction | Increase time at 1800°C |
| Low porosity | PMMA burnout incomplete | Hold longer at 600°C |
| Cracking during sintering | Too fast heating | Reduce heating rate |
| High k (poor insulation) | Porosity too low | Increase pore former |
| Low hardness | Porosity too high | Reduce pore former to 20% |
